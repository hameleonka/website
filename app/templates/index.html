Confronting Climate Change through Cinema

We could tell you “well, we warned you” about global warming. After all, for twenty years we’ve churned out film on climate change and human rights.

But what good is “I told you so” on a burning planet?

At Interlock we’re all about combating climate change enlisting the very best of a filmmaker's craft.
